What is EA Sports RaceNet?

RaceNet from EA Sports is an innovative platform designed to enhance racing experiences, offering a comprehensive suite of features for competitive racing gamers. It provides a centralized hub for tracking race results, comparing stats, and managing leagues across various EA Sports racing titles.

Remote League Management

We will be integrating RaceNet for remote management of our league within the game. This allows for seamless organization of events, monitoring of player progress, and easier communication among league participants.

Track Your Performance

With RaceNet, you can view your league results, compare lap times, setups, and performance on any circuit against friends and racers worldwide. This feature is designed to help you identify areas for improvement and enhance your racing skills.

Accessible Anytime, Anywhere

RaceNet is not just limited to your gaming console or PC. With its dedicated app and website, you can stay connected to your league and racing stats on the go, ensuring you're always ready for your next race.
